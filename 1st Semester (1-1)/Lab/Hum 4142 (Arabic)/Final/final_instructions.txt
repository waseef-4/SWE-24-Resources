- Exam type: Viva
At first, you will be asked to recite from any random ayah from a specific Sura.
Then, you'll be asked 4-5 question (it can differ) from grammars. Frequently asked questions are:
1. Muslimun, Muslimatun table (Heavy version)
2. Concept of Rafa, Nasb, Jarr
3. Flexibility of Ism
4. Concept of Pronouns
5. Independent and Attached pronouns table
6. Harf of Nasb, Jar
7. Idafa
8. Concept of Numbers: Singular, Dual Numbers and Plurals
9. Pointers, Pointers table
10. Mawsoof and Sifa
11. Mubtada and Khabar
12. Types of Sentence - Noun and Verb Based
